Data & ML Enthusiast

Hi, I'm Nabila, most people call me Nab or Bil. I'm someone who’s always juggling a little bit of everything. I learn, i explore, and i find ways to share what i know.

I didn’t always know I’d end up in tech. In college, I sort of stumbled into IT and eventually found my way to machine learning. What surprised me was how much I enjoyed working with data. Numbers started making sense in a way i never expected—they weren’t just data points; they were stories waiting to be told. That realization not only shaped my career but also sparked a sense of wonder I never thought I’d find in tech.

And there's more to me than just codes and spreadsheet. Life, for me, is about finding balance, and I’ve discovered it in the most unexpected places. There’s something grounding about running at sunrise with Arctic Monkeys playing in my ears, then there’s the laughter of kids in rural villages during social programs—those moments remind me of the importance of human connection and the impact of even the smallest acts of kindness. Rr just scribbling my thoughts down on Medium when the world feels overwhelming. Writing, for me, is like talking to myself, but quieter.

Ultimately, my journey is about more than what I do; it’s about how I try to live.
